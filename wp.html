ONIGIERI (GRI) Whitepaper

Project Overview

Token Name: ONIGIERI (GRI)
Network: Solana
Objective: To hold a global popularity vote on Japanese onigiri ingredients and spread Japanese culture worldwide.

Vision and Goals

Vision: The ONIGIERI project aims to promote cultural exchange through onigiri and create international connections via food.

  • Spread Onigiri Culture: To spread the charm of onigiri, a staple of Japanese food culture, worldwide.
  • Build a Global Community: To promote diversity and creativity through ingredient proposals and voting, centered around token holders.
  • Establish a Token Ecosystem: To enhance the value of voting and related events by utilizing ONIGIERI tokens.

Future Prospects

  1. Global Voting Events: Using ONIGIERI tokens, global-scale popularity votes on Japanese onigiri ingredients will be held.
  2. Token Holder Benefits: Special rewards based on voting results, exclusive events, and opportunities to contribute to ingredient development.
  3. Expanding Partnerships: Collaborate with Japanese food manufacturers to turn voting results into products and support ingredient development for overseas markets.
  4. Ecosystem Growth: Expand the token ecosystem by collaborating with Web3 projects and adding new use cases.

Project Value

  • Cultural Exchange: International connections through food culture.
  • Transparency: Management of voting results and token circulation entirely on the blockchain.
  • Participatory Ecosystem: Community-driven project management.
